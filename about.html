About BorrowClever

Who runs this site, and why it exists.

Who runs BorrowClever

BorrowClever is built and run by Luke Watters, an engineer and data enthusiast with a background in technical support and financial systems. The site exists to answer one question properly: what does this credit actually cost? Rates are verified directly against lender websites, and rankings are based on total cost of credit — never commissions.

Why this site exists

Most Irish loan and credit card comparison sites are broker-led: they're paid by lenders to send traffic, and they rank results by whatever makes that arrangement work — often the lowest headline monthly repayment rather than what a loan or card actually costs you over its full term.

BorrowClever ranks personal loans by total cost of credit — the actual interest you'll pay over the life of the loan — and credit cards by representative APR, the standardised metric every Irish issuer is legally required to publish. Lowest first, every time.

It also includes credit unions, which most comparison sites leave out entirely. Over three million people in Ireland are credit union members, and their rates are frequently competitive with — or better than — the banks, particularly for car loans and home improvements. Excluding them from a comparison isn't neutral; it's a gap that happens to favour the lenders who pay for placement elsewhere.

None of that changes how a result is ranked here. See how we make money for the specifics.
