Why BorrowClever

Built to show you what
other sites hide

Most Irish comparison tools are broker-led, exclude credit unions, and rank by monthly repayment — not what borrowing actually costs you.

01

Total cost — always first

Banks advertise low monthly repayments. We show you the total interest you'll pay across the full loan term — the only number that matters.

02

Credit unions included

Bonkers and Switcher compare banks only. We include credit union rates — which are often better for car loans and home improvements.

03

No hidden rankings

Our default sort is always total cost, lowest first. Commission agreements with lenders never influence where a result appears in the list.
